About Vietnamese-German University















The Vietnamese-German University (VGU) is a Vietnamese state university. In 2008, the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the German State Hessen cooperate to establish VGU, which later be broadened to a German-wide cooperation.We follow the successful German model and standards in order to build a research-oriented university of world standard in Ho-Chi-Minh City. Due to high sending experienced professors from Germany and superior German study programs, student will be provided with best chance on the job-market after received a German university degree.VGU provides both lecturers and students with best advantage in excellent study programs and on an internationally competitive level.
